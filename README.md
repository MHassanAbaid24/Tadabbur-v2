# Welcome to Tadabbur 🌿
*Read. Reflect. Grow Together.*

Tadabbur is your personal companion for daily Quranic reflection, designed to help you build a meaningful, lasting relationship with the Quran. Whether you are reflecting alone or growing alongside your family and friends, Tadabbur ensures that your journey with the Quran doesn't stop after Ramadan.

---

## 📖 The Core Experience: Your Daily Habit
Every day, Tadabbur presents you with a new, carefully selected Ayah (verse) that the entire community reflects on together. Taking just 3 minutes a day, you can:
1. **Read & Listen**: Experience the beautiful Uthmani Arabic text (in the elegant Scheherazade New font) accompanied by the English translation and soothing audio recitations.
2. **Understand**: Dive deeper with Ibn Kathir's Tafsir (explanation) available right at your fingertips in an easy-to-read drawer.
3. **Reflect**: Answer two simple, profound prompts: *What does this ayah mean to you, right now?* and *What will you do differently today?*
4. **Grow**: Receive personalized, gently-crafted AI-driven actionable insights based explicitly on your reflections to help you apply the Quran to your daily life.

---

## 📱 Explore The App

### 🏠 Home (Today's Verse)
The heart of Tadabbur. Each day, you are greeted with the Verse of the Day, perfectly synchronized so your loved ones are reading the exact same words.
* **Immersive Verse Card:** View the gorgeous Arabic text and translation.
* **Audio Player:** Tap to listen to the recitation by Mishary Al-Afasy.
* **Tafsir Drawer:** Swipe up to read the Ibn Kathir Tafsir for deeper context and understanding.
* **Reflection & Mood Logging:** Jot down your thoughts, intentions, and tag how the verse made you feel (e.g., peaceful, challenged, grateful).
* **AI Gentle Action:** After you submit your reflection, receive a customized, practical suggestion on how to act upon the verse today.

### 👥 Circles (Grow Together)
Reflection is better together. Overcome the isolation of personal habits by joining a reflection Circle.
* **Create or Join:** Easily create a private group or join your family/friends using a simple invite link.
* **Shared Feed:** Read how others in your Circle reflected on the *exact same* daily verse. 
* **Support & Connect:** Like and react to the reflections of your loved ones to keep each other motivated and accountable.
* *(Privacy First: You always have the toggle to decide whether a reflection is shared with your Circle or kept entirely to yourself.)*

### 📓 Journal (Your Quranic Journey)
A safe, organized space for your heart’s thoughts over time.
* **History Timeline:** Look back at months or years of your personal reflections tied to specific verses and dates.
* **Mood & Memories:** Identify patterns in how different verses uniquely moved you at different points in your life.
* **Full Context:** Tap any past journal entry to re-read the original verse and your thoughts intact.

### 📈 Progress (Gamified Growth)
Building a lasting habit is hard, so we made it rewarding and visually engaging.
* **Daily Streaks:** Watch your streak counter grow by maintaining your daily commitment.
* **Activity Heatmap:** Visualize your consistency over the year with a beautiful calendar heatmap.
* **Earn XP:** Earn Experience Points (XP) for reflecting (+10), sharing (+5), supporting others (+3), and hitting milestone streaks!
* **Level Up Your Rank:** Progress through meaningful tiers as a visual reminder of your dedication:
  * 🌱 **Level 1:** *Seeker (طالب)*
  * 📖 **Level 2:** *Learner (متعلّم)*
  * 🤔 **Level 3:** *Reflector (متدبّر)*
  * ⚙️ **Level 4:** *Practitioner (عامل)*
  * 🌟 **Level 5:** *Guide (مرشد)*

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign Up:** Create a free account to begin your journey.
2. **Connect with Quran Foundation:** Link your official QF account safely and securely to unlock your cross-platform notes, streaks tracking, and community features.
3. **Choose Your Path:** Create a Circle, invite your friends via a link, or choose to journey solo.
4. **Set a Schedule:** Pick a reminder time that fits your life, and we'll remind you to take 3 minutes for your daily reflection.
5. **Start Reflecting:** Open the app and let the Quran speak to you today!

## 💻 Local Development & Quality Automation

To orchestrate frontend and backend development environments seamlessly, Tadabbur provides standard, root-level scripts:

### 1. Launch Development Servers
To start the React frontend (Vite) and Python backend (FastAPI) concurrently:
```bash
npm run dev
```

### 2. Run Test Suites
To run both frontend (Vitest) and backend (Pytest) test suites concurrently:
```bash
npm run test
```

### 3. Verify Static Typing
To run strict compiler checks on the React/TypeScript frontend:
```bash
npm run typecheck
```

### 4. Lint Codebase
To run static analysis checks across the React/TypeScript codebase:
```bash
npm run lint
```
