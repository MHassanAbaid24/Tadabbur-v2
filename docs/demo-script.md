# Tadabbur Demo Script (180 seconds)

## Demo Goal
Show a complete daily Quran habit loop in 3 minutes: read, listen, reflect, share with circle, and track growth.

## 0:00-0:20 | Problem + Vision
**Visual:** Landing on app logo + clean Home screen transition.

**Narration:**  
"Every Ramadan, many of us reconnect with the Quran, then lose consistency after Shawwal. Tadabbur turns Quran reflection into a daily, shared habit: read one ayah, reflect personally, and grow together."

## 0:20-0:50 | Home Experience: Verse, Audio, Tafsir
**Visual:**
- Home page with Arabic verse rendered in Uthmani script
- Translation below Arabic
- Tap Audio play button (Mishary recitation)
- Open Tafsir drawer

**Narration:**  
"Each day, everyone sees the same deterministic verse, so families and friends can reflect together. We present Arabic text, translation, recitation audio, and contextual Tafsir in one focused view."

## 0:50-1:30 | Reflection Submission + AI Companion
**Visual:**
- Fill Prompt 1: meaning for life right now
- Fill Prompt 2: one practical action today
- Select mood
- Submit reflection
- Show returned suggestion card: 'You might also consider...'

**Narration:**  
"Tadabbur moves beyond reading into action. Users answer two guided reflection prompts, save verse-linked journal entries, and receive a gentle practical suggestion for the next 24 hours."

## 1:30-2:00 | Circle Feed & Social Accountability
**Visual:**
- Open Circle tab
- Show member reflections tied to the same verse
- Tap Like on one post
- Show invite flow or invite code briefly

**Narration:**  
"Reflection becomes stronger in community. Private entries stay private, but shared reflections appear in a circle feed where close friends or family can encourage each other."

## 2:00-2:20 | Progress, Streaks, and Heatmap
**Visual:**
- Open Progress page
- Show streak badge
- Show XP bar and level badge
- Scroll/swipe contribution heatmap

**Narration:**  
"Consistency is reinforced through streaks, XP, and level progression. The activity calendar makes daily Quran engagement visible and motivating."

## 2:20-2:45 | Journal Depth & Long-Term Value
**Visual:**
- Open Journal page
- Show historical entries with verse references
- Open one old entry to highlight growth over time

**Narration:**  
"Every reflection is saved as a verse-linked journal entry. Over months, users build a personal record of how the Quran shaped their decisions and character."

## 2:45-3:00 | Technical Close + Judge Callout
**Visual:**
- Quick split view montage: Home, Reflection, Circle, Progress
- End on logo + tagline

**Narration:**  
"Tadabbur integrates Quran Foundation Content and User APIs end-to-end with secure OAuth flows, resilient token handling, and production-ready safeguards. We didn't build another Quran app. We built a daily Quran practice people sustain together."

## Recording Notes
- Keep transitions tight; avoid pauses over 2 seconds.
- Use one prepared account with realistic seeded reflections and streak history.
- Ensure Audio is user-triggered (tap to play) during recording.
